Welcome to Team 3 Diagnostics

From analysis to result. From science to sensitivity. High quality diagnostics are an imperative for better treatment and patient care. Team 3 Hospitals Group, India’s leading healthcare system, has thus far been delivering 3.5 million high-quality diagnostic tests every year, through its Hospitals and Clinics earning an ‘excellent’ rating from 95% of its patients. Team 3 Diagnostics is the result of the ‘good health for all’ mission that is spurring the Team 3 Hospitals Group to touch a billion lives. Following the corporate credo of bringing quality, affordable healthcare closer to the consumer, 2015 saw 100+ Team 3 Diagnostics centres, in 2016 it has been grown to 150+ centres and 2017 landed with 250 + collection centres , 70+ Labs springing up in neighbourhoods across India, delivering expertise that is empowering to doctors and patients alike. Team 3 Diagnostics’ expert technicians and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment are constantly guided by Team 3’s 36-years legacy of excellence to ensure the accuracy and timeliness of test results.
